HEY HEY!
I’m Kaiyu Wang, it’s so lovely meeting you here!

I was 'activated' in China since 1993, now I'm based in Beijing. I went to the University of Nottingham, UK after I was 18, started studying Software Engineering. And after obtaining my BSc Software Engineering 1st class honour’s degree, I chose to keep diving in software system engineering in University College London (UCL) for my masters. Along the way of my studies, I have got myself involved in academic projects including data mining, image segmentation and andriod app development, and have successfully coorperated with Microsoft and Queen Medical Centre Nottingham, etc.

Currently I am working as a software developer (Java mostly) at a medical big-data start-up, striving to build up a system that aims to provide solid solutions for patients under different body situations. Recently I found myself interested in web UX design so now what you see is how a stumbling and tumbling front-end toddler builds up his own portfolio site.

Apart from work, I like to carry my DSLR around to capture great moments flashing by, you could check on my instagram. Also I'm a huge gin-tonic lover, here's my secret recipe: a drizzle of lemon and drink with people you love, the taste will blow you away.
